About Us

Crafted from the Heart

Crochet Heaven was born in 2024 from a simple passion - the meditative joy of turning yarn into something beautiful and wearable. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a small business.

Every item in our shop is handmade by our small team of artisan crocheters. We believe clothing can be both sustainable and stunning, and that the best garments are the ones made with real care and intention.

We source our yarns carefully, favouring natuaral fibres like merino wool, cotton, and alpaca. Our packaging is plastic free, and every purchase helps support independent craft.
