About Us

Crafted from the Heart

Crochet Heaven was born in 2024 from a simple passion - the meditative joy of turning yarn into something beautiful and wearable. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a small business fueled by community and creativity.

Every item in our shop is handmade by our small team of artisan crocheters. We believe clothing can be both sustainable and stunning, and that the best garments are the ones made with real human hands.

We source our yarns carefully, favouring natuaral fibres like merino wool, cotton, and alpaca. Our packaging is plastic free, and every purchase helps support independent craft.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a custom order take?

Custom orders typically take 2-4 weeks from approval, depending on complexity and current workload. Rush orders may be available - just let us know your deadline!

What yarn do you use?

We primarily use high-quality natural fibres including organic cotton, merino wool and alpaca blends sourced from ethical suppliers. Mention any yarn preference or allergies in your custom order form.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship worldwide. Domestic shipping takes 3-5 business days; international orders typically arrive within 7-14 business days. All internationsl orders include full tracking.

How do I care for my crochet pieces

Hamd-wash in cool water with a gentle detergent and lay flat to dry away from direct sunlight. Never tumble dry or wring out crochet items. Detailed care instructions are included with every order.

Can I return or exchange an item?

We accept returns on ready-made items within 14 days of delivery, provided items are unworn and in original condition. Custom orders are non-redundable as they are made specifically for you.
